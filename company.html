Additional Considerations

Tax Services

Professional tax services are required for C-Corporation compliance including:

  • Annual corporate tax returns (Form 1120)
  • Delaware state tax filings
  • Quarterly payroll taxes (if hiring employees)
  • Tax planning and optimization
